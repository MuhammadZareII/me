Physical & Simulation

Device-level physics, electromagnetic field simulation, quantum mechanics, photonic resonator design — the foundation of the stack. These projects are closest to the physics.

Layer philosophy: Physics constrains everything else. Before a photonic device can be fabricated, before a plasmonic waveguide can carry a signal, before a quantum well can emit light — the electromagnetic behaviour must be understood quantitatively. These projects are all simulations and numerical models: FDTD solvers, k·p Hamiltonians, wave-optics propagation engines. They produce no hardware, but they produce understanding.
