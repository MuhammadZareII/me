About Me

I am an Electronics Engineer and a Master’s student in Wireless Communications Engineering (WCE) at the University of Oulu, Finland. My background focuses on embedded hardware and electronics, with experience in analog design, wave simulations, and semiconductor device research. From 2022 to 2024, I worked as an R&D Engineer at Sepidgaman Parseh (biomedical), contributing to the design and development of advanced embedded systems. I hold a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering (Electronics) from the University of Tabriz. My current focus is on reliable hardware and software design for communication systems, supported by research experience in wave simulations and diffractive deep neural networks (D2NNs) for direction-of-arrival estimation.
